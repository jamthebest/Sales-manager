# Privacy Policy for Administrador de Ventas

**Effective Date:** 2025-08-26

Administrador de Ventas ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Administrador de Ventas, we may collect the following types of information:

* **Account Information:** Such as username, password, and profile details.
* **Sales and Business Data:** Information you choose to store in the App (e.g., products, clients, sales records).
* **Device Information:** Such as device type, operating system, and diagnostic data for performance improvements.

We do not collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly provided by you.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

* Operate and provide the core functionality of Administrador de Ventas.
* Synchronize local data with cloud storage for backup and multi-device access.
* Securely authenticate users and manage accounts.
* Improve the App’s performance, fix issues, and add new features.
* Comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

* **Local Storage:** Data is stored on your device to allow offline functionality.

* **Retention:** We keep your data as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements.

---

## 4. Data Deletion

Deleted data will not immediately be erased but may be marked with a deletion timestamp (`deletedAt`) and user identifier (`deletedBy`). This ensures accurate synchronization across devices while preventing deleted items from being displayed in your account.

---

## 5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may only be shared in the following cases:

* **Service Providers:** With third-party providers who assist in operating the App.
* **Legal Compliance:** When required by applicable laws or regulations.

---

## 6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Administrador de Ventas is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.
